EARLY FOUNDATIONS

Before I entered the world of startups and fintech, I built games. I completed schooling and explored multiple academic paths, but my true education happened in Unity and C#.

I built and published 8–10 mobile games on the Play Store and App Store.

LESSONS IN SYSTEMS

Game development gave me a systems-level way of thinking that still shapes how I build products today. When you build a game, you deal with complex state management, physics loops, and performance constraints on low-end devices. These constraints breed creativity.
